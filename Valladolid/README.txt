--- Prefix: "vd!"


--- Setting position: Prefix + "x"
	+++ Example: "vd!10"
	 	--- Sets position to 10th 


--- Setting points: Prefix + "points" + "x"
	+++ Example: "vd!points 100"
		--- Sets points to 100










NOTE: "x" uvdd in the examples can be any desired by you number.
